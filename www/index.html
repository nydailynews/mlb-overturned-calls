Crusty old baseball people would frequently utter the cliche 'it's better to be lucky than good.'

Prior to 2014, that was probably true. But since MLB implemented replay review, luck doesn't really factor in anymore.

So far in 2018 calls have been overturned at a rate of just under 50%, which is in line with previous years.

Another constant as been the Yankees successful call overturn rate. The bombers have ranked in the top three in MLB with winning challenges since 2014, owning a 68% success rate. The Mets however has been about as inconsistent as their record in the standings.

So far this year, the Mets are in the bottom third of all MLB teams in successful challenges, coming in at 42%. Their best year was 2015 where they ranked just below the Royals, with 67%.
